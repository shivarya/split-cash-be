Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 5, 2025

Welcome to Split Cash! This Privacy Policy explains how ShivArya ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Our Commitment: We believe in transparency and keeping your data safe. We only collect what's necessary to provide our expense sharing service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Split Cash, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture (provided via Google Sign-In)
  • Expense Data: Groups you create or join, expenses you add, and settlement records
  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers for authentication purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your Split Cash account
  • Enable expense sharing and tracking with your groups
  • Calculate balances and suggested settlements
  • Send email notifications (group invitations, expense updates)
  • Improve our app's functionality and user experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared only in these cases:

  • With Group Members: Your name and expenses are visible to members of groups you join
  • Service Providers: We use Google Sign-In for authentication
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Security

We take security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Passwords are never stored (we use Google OAuth)
  • Database access is restricted and protected
  • We regularly review our security practices

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your expense data

7. Children's Privacy

Split Cash is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication. See Google's Privacy Policy
